ABOUT US

South India's
Largest IT and Digital
Retail Chain

Gadget Galaxy, the digital shop is South India’s most reputed and highly rated retail chain for digital products.It has long standing associations with the leading brands of the digital world. Almost a decade ago, when the digital revolution swept across Kerala, GG Digital Shop rode the crest of this revolutionary wave by bringing customers the latest technology products and fully meeting customer expectations and aspirations. Today, GG enjoys the firm loyalty of over 30,00,000 customers.The genesis GG’s triumphant journey began way back in 2010, when it opened shop as Ozone Systems in the hilltown of Kanjirappilly. Since then, there has been no looking back. From the retail and distribution of IT peripherals, the organization grew exponentially with the driving strength of a visionary leadership.GG the digital shop took birth. Customers were introduced to a stunning range of smartphones, laptops and in a world class shopping ambience. The product portfolio expanded to GG became perceived as a ‘One Stop Digital Shop’. The shops were manned by highly experienced sales personnel who knew the art of fulfilling customer expectations.Expansion mode Of late, GG has been on an expansion spree.
